About Us

Mission Statement: At REVEL MOTOR SALES, we strive to provide unparalleled services and products that meet the evolving needs of our customers. Our focus is on quality, customer satisfaction, and creating a positive impact within our community.

History: Founded in 1990, REVEL MOTOR SALES began as a small business with a clear vision: to bring the best quality of any type of vehicle we sell to the automobile industry. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted name, offering a variety of high-quality products and services.
